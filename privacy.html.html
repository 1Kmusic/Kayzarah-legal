Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Kay Zarah Hookah Bar ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Kay Zarah mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Account login information
  • Order history
  • Room and event booking information
  • Device location (only when permission is granted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process food and drink orders
  • Manage bookings
  • Provide loyalty points and membership benefits
  • Send order confirmations
  • Send promotional push notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve customer service

3. Payments

Payments are processed through third-party providers including MonCash, NatCash, and card networks (Visa/Mastercard). We do NOT store card numbers on our servers.

4. Location Data

Location is used only to improve delivery accuracy and is collected only if you grant permission.

5. Push Notifications

You may receive notifications regarding orders or promotions. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate the app.

7. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes.

8. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by contacting us at KayZarahHookahBar@gmail.com. All personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
